👥 Who Can We Help?
✅ ELIGIBLE
  • Medicare Part A + B
  • Age 50 to 89
  • Living at home independently
  • Makes own decisions
  • Has Part C (can upgrade)
❌ NOT ELIGIBLE — Hang Up
  • VA Benefits
  • TRICARE
  • Job retiree / employer coverage
  • Nursing home / assisted living
  • Age 49 or below
  • Age 90 or above
  • POA not available
⚡ Critical Call Rules
IMMEDIATE HANGUP: Abusive language • "Remove me from list" (DNC) • "Is this a scam?" • VA/TRICARE/Employer • Nursing home • Age 49 or below / 90 or above
MAX 2 REBUTTALS: After 2 rebuttals → "Have a wonderful day!" → HANGUP. Never try 3rd rebuttal.
DISCLAIMER before transfer: "I'm going to connect this call to a licensed agent, and even if your number is listed on any kind of federal or state do not call list, you are consenting to receive this call. Do you understand and agree to proceed?" → MUST get YES or OKAY!
