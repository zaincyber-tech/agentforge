📋 Medicare Parts — What You Must Know
A
Hospital Insurance
Covers inpatient hospital stays, nursing facility care, hospice, and some home health services.
B
Medical Insurance
Covers doctor visits, outpatient care, preventive services, and medical equipment.
C
Medicare Advantage WE SELL
Private plans covering everything A+B PLUS dental, vision, hearing, OTC allowances — at no extra cost to customer!
D
Prescription Drugs
Covers prescription medications. We do NOT sell standalone Part D plans.
Supplement
Medigap — We do NOT sell this
If customer has ONLY Medigap — we cannot help them. Move on.

👥 Who Can We Help?
✅ ELIGIBLE — Continue Call
  • Medicare Part A + Part B enrolled
  • Age 50 to 89 years old
  • Living independently at home
  • Makes own healthcare decisions
  • Has Part C — can compare/upgrade
❌ NOT ELIGIBLE — Hang Up Now
  • Has VA Benefits
  • Has TRICARE
  • Has job retiree / employer coverage
  • Living in nursing home / assisted living
  • Age 49 or below
  • Age 90 or above
  • POA not available to speak
⚡ Critical Call Rules
IMMEDIATE HANGUP — NO WORDS:
• Customer uses abusive language or profanity
• "Remove me from list" / "Don't call me again" (DNC)
• "Is this a scam?" / Asks if this is telemarketing
• Has VA / TRICARE / Employer coverage
• In nursing home or assisted living
• Age 49 or below — Age 90 or above
MAX 2 REBUTTALS ONLY: If customer says not interested — try 2 rebuttals max. After 2nd rebuttal if still no → "Have a wonderful day!" → HANGUP. Never attempt 3rd rebuttal.
DISCLAIMER before EVERY transfer: "I'm going to connect this call to a licensed agent, and even if your number is listed on any kind of federal or state do not call list, you are consenting to receive this call. Do you understand and agree to proceed?" → MUST get YES or OKAY before transferring!
